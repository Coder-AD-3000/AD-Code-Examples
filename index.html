The Mystery Stone

The picture to the right, shows the mysterious stone known only by the moniker ‘The Mystery Stone’. Although unassuming in appearance, and arguably outwardly bland by nature, no-one actually knows where the stone came from. Nor, apparently, does anyone really care. That was, however, until a consortium of bored, yet intrepid, archeological researchers at a respected University (some speculate, Harvard) decided to spend almost two entire minutes studying and poring over every little detail contained within the ponderous photo. Despite this, however, the stone’s mysterious secrets remain just that – an unforgiving enigma. Significantly, in 2018, some particularly rabble-rousing (basically good-for-nothing) Student Union student dissenters, boldly speculated that ‘only the pixels know’ the true story of ‘that worthless, crappy, little stone’. On one occasion, famously, when asked about the stone on live TV, the serial tech-entrepreneur Elon Musk once nonchalantly stated: “I don’t know where the mystery stone came from, it’s a mystery.”
